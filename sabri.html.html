EVENT CEREMONY OVERVIEW

Welcome to The EASTC Seventh Annually Convocation Ceremony where it done annually before the graduation ceremony as special occassion to honor our graduating students for their hard work and dedication. Students are provided with awards and certification due to their class performance which encourages to increase more effort to students in studying hard and attaining awards. You're all Welcome.

The last year's convocation theme stated"Modernesing data ecosystems to accelerate the implementaion of African Continental Free Trade(AfCTA). The role of official statistics and big data in the economic trnsformation and sustainable development in Tanzania"

The ceremony meeting will be held on;

Date: 3thDecember 2024

Time: 9:00-15:30

Location: EASTC Functional Hall
